Klaus Hargreeves

Character Information

Character Information

  • Date of Birth: October 1st 1989
  • Aliases: Number 4
  • Power: Speaks With/Summons the Dead
  • Description: Klaus Hargreeves is one of the forty-three children who were all born on the same day in 1989 to a mother without any previous signs of pregnancy. The baby was one of seven adopted by Sir Reginald Hargreeves with the intention of training them to save the world. Ranked by their individual usefulness, Klaus was Hargreeves' Number Four with the ability to speak to the dead. Klaus is played by Robert Sheehan in the Netflix adaptation of The Umbrella Academy.
