Luther Hargreeves

Number 1

  • Date of Birth: October 1st 1989
  • Aliases: Number 1
  • Power: Super Strength
  • Description: Luther Hargreeves is one of the forty-three children who were all born on the same day in 1989 to a mother without any previous signs of pregnancy. The baby was one of seven adopted by Sir Reginald Hargreeves with the intention of training them to save the world. Ranked by their individual usefulness, Luther was Hargreeves' Number One and the leader of The Umbrella Academy. He possesses super strength and durability.
